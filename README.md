Race Day Scheduler (JavaScript)

This is a simple JavaScript program that assigns race numbers and race times based on age and registration status.
If a runner is over 18 and registered early, they get a special race number and race at 9:30 AM.
If they are over 18 but registered late, they race at 11:00 AM.
If they are under 18, they race at 12:30 PM.
The program uses conditions (if-else) and random numbers to generate race numbers.
It helps practice JavaScript logic and decision making.
